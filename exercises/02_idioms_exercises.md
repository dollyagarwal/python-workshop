# Exercises: Pythonic Idioms

1. **Comprehensions**  
   Given `[1,2,3,4,5]`, produce squares for even numbers only.

2. **Enumerate/Zip**  
   Combine two lists `names` and `scores` into a dict `{name: score}`.

3. **Unpacking**  
   Given a tuple `(id, name, *rest)`, unpack safely and ignore extra values.

4. **Context Manager**  
   Read lines from `data.txt`, strip whitespace, and write to `clean.txt` using `with`.

5. **Itertools**  
   Use `itertools.groupby` to group items by a key function.
